Reinforcement Learning in Logic Synthesis

(view repo)

The project considers a Markov Decision Process formulation of the logic synthesis problem, as proposed by earlier works.

A state representation is engineered to efficiently represent and-inverter graphs

An RL agent is trained for the task of providing synthesis recipes.

Performance of synthesis recipes derived from such an RL model is examined in later stages of FPGA CAD flow.

Performance comparison of CNN and LSTM Architectures for Environmental Sound Classification

(view repo)

Preprocessed audio samples from UrbanSound8K, representing them as Mel Spectrograms for machine learning purposes.

Conducted preliminary experiments on different ResNet (CNN) and LSTM architectures over a smaller number of epochs, with the goal of selecting candidates for further consideration.

In the final experiment round, performed 10-fold validation over a larger number of epochs, evaluating selected candidates against each other.
